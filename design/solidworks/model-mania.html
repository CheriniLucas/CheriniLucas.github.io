Model Mania!

Model Mania is a design challenge held since 2000 that measures the modeling skills, both in accuracy and time, of engineers and designers who wish to compete and demonstrate their SolidWorks skills during the annual Dassault Systemes conference.

In 2008, structural analysis of the part was added to determine its safety factor and increase the difficulty of the test.

These are all the designs made by me to date.
