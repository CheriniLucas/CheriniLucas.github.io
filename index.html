About me

I am Lucas Cherini, master builder and advanced student of Mechanical Engineering.

Previously, I worked in the construction industry as a senior master builder, where I consulted, designed, managed and built domestic works and installations.

At the same time, I worked in the manufacturing industry as a Methods and Time Analyst. There I was in charge of the development, control and optimization of the manufacturing processes of the Alcatel ONE TOUCH lines; and as assistant of the Motorola, Noblex, Lenovo and Sony lines.

I am currently working at the Aerospace Technology Center as a piping designer, developing 3D models, piping plans, isometries, layouts, piping class and stress analysis for the new rocket engine test bench for the Tronador II project.

I have experience in design and simulation in AutoCAD, AutoCAD Plant 3D, SolidWorks, Catia, Abaqus, Caesar II, MatLab, C, Python and Arduino.
